Why

Can't decide where to eat? Can't decide which phone to buy?

Which subject should you study first? Which show should you binge-watch first?

Don't let the paradox of choice slow get you down.

Lose all worry; let UNIVOICE pick a choice!

How

It gets a random number from atmospheric noise data. All of your choices are assigned an id, and the choice whose id matches the random number is picked.

Simply add in all of your choices. Then click on CHOOSE FOR ME! to pick a choice.

You are also shown a cool "fun fact" about the number of your choice. Neat!
